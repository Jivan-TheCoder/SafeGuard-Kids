Introduction

By utilizing our services, you formally represent and warrant that you possess the inherent legal authority to integrate monitoring software onto the target device.

Authorized Device Integration

  • The suite must be deployed in strict accordance with our official technical guides.

  • You maintain full responsibility for the physical security and data integrity of the monitored device.
