Digital Sovereignty Manifesto

This Privacy Policy Protocol governs the acquisition, transmission, and terminal storage of all data processed within the SafeGuard Kids ecosystem. We operate under a "Zero-Trust" architectural framework, where the security of the family unit is the absolute priority. Engagement with our platform—including software installation and service subscription—constitutes an explicit, legally-binding agreement to these rigorous safety conditions.

Our operational commitment is binary: We provide comprehensive protective tools for legal guardians, while ensuring third-party entities—including advertisers, brokers, and unauthorized agencies—have zero visibility into your family's private digital interactions.

Strict "Guardian-Only" Mandatory Use Policy

SafeGuard Kids is engineered exclusively for the monitoring of minor children by their legitimate legal guardians. Any attempt to utilize this software for the surveillance of non-consenting adults—including, but not limited to, spouses, girlfriends, boyfriends, domestic partners, employees, or other associates—is a direct, fundamental violation of this agreement and applicable international laws.

By using this platform, you explicitly represent and warrant that the target of monitoring is a minor for whom you hold primary legal responsibility. Misuse of the platform for adult tracking is defined as a "Material Breach" that subjects the user to immediate, non-refundable termination and potential reporting to local law enforcement agencies.

Absolute Indemnification & Liability Shield

The user acknowledges that SafeGuard Kids (the "Service Provider") provides a technical tool and does not control the user's intent or application. To the maximum extent permitted by law, you agree to:

  • Indemnification: Defend, indemnify, and hold harmless SafeGuard Kids and its directors, employees, and affiliates from and against any and all claims, damages, obligations, losses, liabilities, costs, or debt, and expenses (including but not limited to attorney's fees) resulting from your misuse of the software or breach of the Guardian-Only policy.
  • Liability Limitation: In no event shall SafeGuard Kids be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, including without limitation, loss of privacy, loss of data, or legal repercussions arising from unauthorized surveillance conducted by the user.
  • User Warranty: You accept all legal responsibility for determining whether your monitoring is lawful in your specific jurisdiction.

Mandatory Legal Capacity & Authentication

To register and maintain an active subscription, you must be at least 18 years of age and possess full legal capacity to enter into binding contracts. You affirm that you hold valid, documented legal guardianship over the minor monitored. SafeGuard Kids reserves the unilateral right to request legal documentation (e.g., birth certificates or court orders) to verify the guardian-child relationship if any suspicious monitoring patterns are detected by our automated Safety Audit systems.

Prohibited Surveillance & Stalking Protocols

We maintain an exhaustive list of prohibited activities. Any account found engaging in the following will be subject to the Hard-Lock Protocol:

  • Unauthorized Adult Monitoring: Installing the software on a device owned by an adult without their explicit, written, and revocable consent (where permitted by law).
  • Harassment & Cyber-Stalking: Utilizing high-precision GPS telemetry to facilitate the physical harassment or stalking of any individual.
  • Stealth Surveillance of Non-Minors: Any attempt to bypass the notification layers of the software to monitor non-minors covertly.
  • Commercial Espionage: Tracking competitors or employees for business gain.

Zero-Tolerance Termination & Non-Refund Policy

SafeGuard Kids operates under a Zero-Tolerance Framework for policy violations. If our Safety Audit systems or any reported evidence suggests that the platform is being used to track non-minors or for other illicit purposes:

  • The account will be Hard-Locked immediately, severing all data streams.
  • All related data on our servers will be subjected to the **Emergency Purge Protocol** to prevent further misuse.
  • The user's subscription will be terminated **without refund**.
  • The user may be permanently blacklisted from all SafeGuard-affiliated services.

Data Processing & Attribution Hierarchy

We categorize all incoming telemetry into three distinct processing tiers, each governed by specific security sub-protocols to ensure maximum privacy for the child and parent:

  • Tier-Alpha (Interactive Content): Includes the raw character streams from monitored social platforms (WhatsApp, Instagram). This data is processed locally on the target device via accessibility filters and transmitted through encrypted tunnels.
  • Tier-Beta (Geospatial & Telemetry): Real-time high-precision GPS coordinates, cellular signal strength, and device battery status. This data is "Ephemeral" and is overwritten every 24 hours.
  • Tier-Gamma (Parental Credentials): Your account metadata and billing info. This data is isolated on our most secure core infrastructure.

Cryptographic Infrastructure (Military-Grade)

SafeGuard Kids leverages the most advanced commercially available encryption standards to ensure that data remains impenetrable to unauthorized entities:

  • At-Rest: All logs stored on our cloud infrastructure are secured with AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard). Keys are rotated every 48 hours via an automated KMS.
  • In-Transit: All data flowing from the child's device to the parent dashboard is protected by TLS 1.3 with perfect forward secrecy.
  • Identity Layer: Parental credentials are hashed using SHA-256 with unique cryptographic salts.

Internal Lockdown & The "Zero-Access" Guarantee

A critical pillar of our insurance-grade privacy framework is the Internal Lockdown Protocol. Our engineering and support teams possess zero technical capability to view, intercept, or decrypt raw Tier-Alpha content. Cryptographic keys are generated and stored at a level that is mathematically inaccessible to any SafeGuard employee or third-party contractor.

Global Regulatory Compliance (COPPA, GDPR, CCPA)

We maintain meticulous compliance with international data safety frameworks to ensure global authoritative standing:

  • COPPA: We exceed COPPA requirements by ensuring that no data is collected for commercial or advertising purposes.
  • GDPR: Every user retains the "Right to be Forgotten." You may initiate a Purge Protocol at any time via your secondary dashboard.
  • CCPA: We provide full transparency on what data is collected and ensure a Zero-Sale Policy for all California residents.

Terminal Data Retention & Automated Purge Schedules

We adhere to a "Terminal Retention" philosophy. Data is never stored indefinitely. Our automated systems enforce a 30-day Purge Cycle for all content logs. After 30 days, any information not flagged as a "Critical Safety Event" is mathematically destroyed and is unrecoverable even by our forensic engineering team.

Third-Party Disclosure Framework

SafeGuard Kids does not sell, trade, or leak your data. We do not cooperate with advertising agencies. The only exceptions are:

  • Infrastructure Providers: Secure hosting environments (e.g., AWS/Google Cloud) who hold data in an encrypted, non-viewable state.
  • Legal Warrants: We only provide information when compelled by a valid, legally-binding court order. Even then, we can only provide encrypted headers; we cannot provide the content of private chats if they have been purged.
