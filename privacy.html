Data Commitment

At SafeGuard Kids, we believe that your family's digital sovereignty is non-negotiable. This Privacy Policy serves as our manifesto on how we acquire, secure, and utilize data to facilitate a safer digital environment for your children.

Our operational commitment is binary: We provide full-spectrum defensive tools for parents, while ensuring third-party entities have zero visibility into your family's private interactions.

Essential Data Acquisition & Intended Utility

Our data collection is exclusively purpose-driven. We only process information that is computationally necessary to power the 'Safe-Pro' defensive suite:

  • Encrypted Social Monitoring: Processing interactions on encrypted platforms (WhatsApp, Instagram) to detect threats such as cyberbullying and predatory solicitation.
  • Defensive Communication Logs: Logging metadata for incoming/outgoing calls to identify unrecognized or high-risk contacts immediately.
  • Geospatial Safety Stream: Delivering high-precision, real-time GPS coordinates to ensure your child's physical security is always verified.

Pro-Active Defense & Military-Grade Encryption

We leverage industrial-grade AES-256 bit encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Every piece of family data is compartmentalized and secured with cryptographic keys that are unique to your parent dashboard. Critically, our engineering team cannot view private chat content; the data is accessible only to the authorized account holder.

Mandatory Guardian Consent & Transparency

SafeGuard Kids is strictly an 'Opt-In' safety tool. Operation requires legitimate legal guardianship and an explicit technical configuration by the parent on the target device. We strictly prohibit the use of our infrastructure for any form of unauthorized or covert adult surveillance.

Data Lifecycle & Automated Purge Schedules

We adhere to a 'Minimal Retention' philosophy. Family data is never stored indefinitely; logs and telemetry are subjected to automated secure purge cycles (typically 30–90 days), after which they are mathematically unrecoverable from our production servers.

Absolute Zero-Sharing & Data Privacy Guarantee

Your data is never for sale. SafeGuard Kids maintains an absolute firewall between your family's data and third-party advertisers or brokers. We do not participate in commercial data exchange; our revenue model is purely subscription-based to ensure our interests remain aligned with your family's privacy.

Regulatory Alignment & Global Safety Standards

We maintain rigorous compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and international data safety frameworks. Our development roadmaps are continuously audited to ensure we exceed global standards for minor protection and data safety.
