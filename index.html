FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is the monitoring app legal to use?

Yes, our app is legal when used to monitor minor children as a parent or guardian, or with explicit consent. We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding parental monitoring software.

Will my child know they're being monitored?

You can choose between visible and stealth mode. However, we recommend discussing digital safety with your children to build trust while ensuring their protection.

Do I need physical access to my child's phone?

Yes, a one-time physical access is required to install and configure the application. After setup, all monitoring is done remotely via your personal dashboard.

How secure is my child's data?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all data storage and transmission. Your family's privacy is our top priority.

Can I monitor multiple devices?

Yes, depending on your plan, you can monitor multiple devices from a single parent account. Check our pricing section for multi-device support details.

What platforms are supported?

SafeGuard currently supports all major Android smartphones and tablets (version 5.0 and above). iOS support is coming soon.
